Welcome to Silhouette Salon AND Barber Suites!

Silhouette Salon and Barber Suites are the premiere beauty and barber suites in the Chattanooga, Cleveland, and Dalton, GA areas. These suites will be the most sought after locations for stylists and barbers looking for a fresh, upscale, private experience for their clients. Create the salon suite of your dreams. Our high-end build outs include features such as oversized sliding glass doors with lock, floor-to-ceiling walls to allow you privacy, premium lighting to emulate natural light, individual ventilation in each suite, and more. As part of our ongoing commitment to help you succeed, we will set you up with your own personal Silhouette Website Profile free of charge, exclusive access to our SilhouGenius booking engine to help fill your books, access to our all-in-one support and educational resources on our Silhouette Pro App, and more.

Why Should I Have a Salon Suite?

Our salon suites are designed to provide your clients with a luxurious, relaxing and personalized experience. You, the stylist, are a highly skilled professional and we know you’re dedicated to making your clients look and feel their best. Taking them into your world in your very own private suite will give them a whole new experience that’s different from the usual room full of stylists and clients where everyone is chatting and socializing. Being able to offer them this quiet, personal, one on one environment will keep them coming back!

We've designed our salon suites with your comfort and convenience in mind. The spacious and elegant environment ensures a relaxing experience. Each station is equipped with state-of-the-art tools and products to provide you with the best possible service. Our shampoo bowls, hair dryers, and styling stations are meticulously maintained to ensure your safety and satisfaction.
